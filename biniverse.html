About BINI

Bini is a Filipino pop girl group with 8 members: Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Sheena, and Jhoanna. The group was formed through a reality TV show called Star Hunt Academy (SHA). They debuted under ABS-CBN's Star Music on June 11, 2021 with their debut single "Born to Win".

BINI members' debut photoshoot

Facts about BINI

  1. BINI came from the tagalog word "binibini" which means young lady.
  2. Their pre-debut song is Da Coconut Nut which is a song that was popularized by the Filipino band "Smokey Mountain" in the 90s.
  3. The members were trained by both Filipino and Korean coaches in singing and dancing.
  4. Their leader Jhoanna, is in fact the second to the youngest member in the group.
